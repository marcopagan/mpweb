Il Deserto dei Tartari

Year Medium Clients Collaborators
2021 book ISIA U Emma Gabrielli, Susanna Tomassini

Il Deserto dei Tartari is a novel written by Dino Buzzati. The plot of the novel is Drogo’s lifelong wait for a great war in which his life as a soldier and the existence of the fort can prove its usefulness. The human need for giving life a meaning and the soldier’s desire for glory are themes in the novel.

To create a visual parallel narration to the novel, we used Gaetano Kanitzsa’s illustrations. Extrapolated from his gestalt psychology’s books, they get a new meaning, amplificating the most illusory and reflective moments in Drogo’s life. In the same way Drogo tries to find a meaning in his meaningless wait, the reader is led to search for meaning in the ambiguos images.

We also used typography in an expressive way. We created two variations of Messina Serif, inspired by Kanitzsa’s illustrations, for Drogo’s pensive moments. A sans font is used for the more disillusioned moments.
